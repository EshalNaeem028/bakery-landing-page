Our Menu

"Our menu is a delightful journey through the world of baked goods. From classic favorites to innovative creations, we offer a wide range of pastries, cakes, breads, and more. Each item is made with love and attention to detail, ensuring that every bite is a moment of pure bliss. Explore our menu and discover your new favorite treat!"

Pastries
Discover the art of the perfect laminate. Our pastries feature hundreds of micro-layers of premium butter and flour, baked to a deep golden brown. Enjoy our signature almond croissants, pain au chocolat, glazed fruit danishes, and savory morning buns spun with fresh cinnamon sugar.
Cakes
Crafted for celebration, big or small. We specialize in multi-layered masterpiece cakes featuring silky Swiss meringue buttercream, rich ganaches, and scratch-made fruit compotes. Choose from timeless favorites like Velvet Vanilla and Decadent Fudge, or order a custom design tailored entirely to your event.
Breads
Baked fresh every single morning before the sun comes up. Our bread selection features wild-yeast sourdoughs, rustic French baguettes, soft brioche loaves, and hearty whole-grain rounds. We use a slow, 24-hour fermentation process to develop deep flavor, a beautiful open crumb, and a perfect, crackling crust.
